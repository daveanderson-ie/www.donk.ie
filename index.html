We make bills simple

Remember when you bought milk from the milkman, vegetables from the grocer, meat from the butcher, fabric from the haberdasher ...?

Now think about how you buy services. Phone from the phone company, electricity from the electricity company, home insurance from the home insurance company.

It’s all online now, so you simply log on to each of their convenient digital portals, with your password. No not that one. It needs at least one digit. No, remember you changed it? Oh wait, didn’t you switch energy suppliers last winter? No, your phone is in himself’s name. No we can’t deal with you.

It’s all so convenient. For them.

Now imagine all of that in one place. All your bills, where you (and your better half) can see them. Paid at a time of the month that works for you. Sent to you in the post. Yes, an actual letter through your letterbox, with all your bills and details in one place. And if we find a better service provider, we’ll let you know, and you can switch without filling in any forms.

This is how services should work.
